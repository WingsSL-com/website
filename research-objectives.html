Research Objectives

01

Bird Visual Identification

  • Overcome limitations of image recognition in identifying birds in various postures and angles.
  • Enhance accuracy and processing speed.
  • Provide a summarized updated description of the identified bird.
  • Build custom lists of the birds likely to be spotted according to the location.

02

Bird Acoustic Identification

  • Identifying individual birds within flocks or distant scenarios
  • Utilizes advanced audio processing technology to capture and analyze bird songs exclusively
  • Filters out background noises effectively, ensuring accurate identification.
  • Capable of identifying individual bird species within mixed flocks, providing a comprehensive understanding of bird communities.
  • User-friendly interface for easy recording and identification processes.

03

Bird Behavior Analysis

  • Crucial Role in Communication: Bird vocalizations serve as essential means of communication, conveying messages like mating signals, territorial defense, and alarm responses.
  • Insights into Behavior: The diverse range of bird vocalizations offers valuable insights into avian behavior and ecological interactions.
  • Challenges in Analysis: Comprehensive analysis and classification of these vocalizations pose challenges due to their intricate patterns, necessitating advanced methodologies.

04

Birds Habitat Insight

  • Visually engaging statistics graphs that present data on bird habitats, such as geographical distribution, environmental conditions, and population trends.
  • The function analyzes historical data on bird habitats to identify fluctuations in population as well as their habitats.
  • Utilizing advanced forecasting techniques, the function predicts future population trends based on current data trends, environmental factors, and historical patterns.
