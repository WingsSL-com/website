Research Problem & Solution

01

Proposed Problem

How to overcome challenges in biodiversity monitoring, which ensures accurate recognition of birds in diverse postures, analyzing real-field bird songs amidst noisy backgrounds, capturing various bird call types to enhance comprehension, and addressing urgent issues of climate change impacts and alarming species loss through advanced forecasting?

02

Proposed Solution

The proposed solution aims to develop an advanced AI-powered platform specifically designed to support stakeholders involved in the conservation, study, and sustainability of bird populations in Sri Lanka. By leveraging the latest advancements in computer vision, machine learning, and data mining, the platform will provide an intelligent, interactive system capable of automatically identifying bird species with high accuracy, regardless of variations in posture or environmental conditions. The integration of these technologies will also allow for the behavioral analysis of birds, generating comprehensive profiles for each species, which include data on movement patterns, vocalizations, and habitat preferences.

Additionally, the platform will offer real-time data visualization tools such as interactive dashboards, which will present users with detailed insights into environmental factors, habitat conditions, and bird population trends. By employing forecasting models, the system will provide regional-level predictions about future bird populations and habitat stability, helping conservationists focus on critical areas in need of protection. This forecasting capability is particularly important for understanding how climate change, deforestation, and other environmental threats may impact avian biodiversity over time.
