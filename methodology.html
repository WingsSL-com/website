Methodology

Our proposed solution to the main problem, as shown below, is made up of four major functions. Inside the Nginx server, we are going to use CNN, LSTM, and KNN machine- learning algorithms. The Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) is used in conjunction with the transfer learning model to obtain accurate bird identification results. It will assist in obtaining the most accurate bird identification and providing a summary description in a timely and accurate manner by examining images that the user uploaded into the system. Besides, the Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) is used with spectrograms to record bird species audio recordings with minimum noise for training data and also, extract features from the spectrogram, capturing relevant patterns. Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) is used with chromagrams in machine learning for noise cancellation to improve signal quality by reducing or eliminating unwanted noise to accurately identify and distinguish specific behavioral call types such as mating, hunger, fear, etc. within bird vocalizations and mapping entire spectral sound data into one octave. In addition to that, the K-Nearest Neighbor (KNN) algorithm is used to update the data anytime, without training the model and also, make predictions by implementing Euclidean distance to get a forecast about bird populations.
